Aracaju Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name: Aracaju Chamber

The site name was selected to represent the city in which the chamber operates. It reflects the mission of connecting businesses and building communities.

Site Purpose

The Aracaju Chamber of Commerce serves as a central hub for entrepreneurs, fostering collaboration, and providing opportunities for business growth. The website aims to:

  • Providing information about business events and networking opportunities.
  • Offering resources for economic and community development.

Scenarios

Here are three scenarios that the website aims to address:

  1. How can the chamber support my business?
  2. What educational resources are available to help entrepreneurs enhance their skills?
  3. What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The color scheme reflects the organization's purpose and trademark. The selected colors are:

  • Primary Color: #264653 (used for headings)
  • Secondary Color: #2A9D8F (used for background accents)
  • Third Color: #E9C46A (used for footer and boxes)

Typography

The chosen font for the website is Raleway and Open Sans. It will be used as follows:

  • Headings: Raleway
  • Body Text: Raleway
  • Buttons Text: Open Sans
